Incident Communication

Available on all plans
Manual P0 and P1 incident trigger

Raise a critical incident in seconds directly within Composure or via your connected PSA tool. Communications begin immediately.

Severity escalation handling

As severity escalates, Composure adjusts tone, urgency, and communication frequency automatically — keeping clients appropriately informed.

Duplicate incident detection

Composure identifies when multiple alerts relate to the same underlying event, preventing clients from receiving confusing duplicate communications.

Multi-incident dashboard

A clear, real-time view of all active incidents and their communication status across every affected client. No incidents fall through the gaps.

Communication playbooks

Define your communication rules — what language to avoid, what level of technical detail to share, what tone to use with which clients — and Composure follows them every time.

Private internal notes

Keep internal context, notes, and updates completely separate from client-facing communications. Your internal deliberations never accidentally reach a client.

Scheduled proactive holding updates

Composure automatically schedules regular updates so clients know they haven't been forgotten, even when there's no new resolution progress to share.

Communication pause and resume

In sensitive or rapidly evolving situations, pause all outbound communications with one click. Resume with full context preserved when the time is right.

Sentiment and language checks before send

Composure reviews every draft before it leaves — checking for blame language, oversharing of technical detail, or tone that doesn't match your playbook standards.

Stakeholder & Contact Management

Available on all plans
Customer and contact management

Maintain a complete record of every client, contact, role, and communication preference. All context Composure needs to write the right message for the right person.

Role-based contacts and preferences

Tag each contact with their role — CTO, Finance Director, IT Manager, Operations — and define their preferred channel, level of detail, and communication frequency.

Audience-calibrated AI messaging

Each message is crafted specifically for its recipient's role — not the same message adjusted slightly, but genuinely different content, tone, and level of detail.

Per-contact previews and editing

Before sending, preview exactly what each contact will receive. Edit individual messages without affecting others. Full human control before anything is sent.

Acknowledgement tracking

See who has opened and acknowledged each communication. Know immediately if a key stakeholder hasn't seen a critical update.

Failed delivery detection and retry

If a communication fails to deliver, Composure detects it immediately, alerts your team, and automatically retries via an alternative channel.

Internal Briefing & AM Support

Communicate & Platform
AM and CSM internal briefings

Automatically brief Account Managers and Customer Success Managers at the same time client communications go out — so your relationship team is always ahead of the client's call.

Internal update chasing

Composure prompts engineers for status updates at the right intervals, using canned-response shortcuts to make internal updates fast and frictionless.

Ticket and incident data capture

Composure pulls relevant ticket context from your PSA automatically, so the AI has the raw information it needs to draft accurate, contextual communications.

Planned & Routine Communications

Communicate & Platform
General communications

Send professional service updates, product notices, and general communications to selected clients or contacts — all managed through Composure with the same quality and consistency.

Planned maintenance notices

Schedule and send structured maintenance communications — initial notice, reminder, in-progress update, and completion confirmation — all managed as a single communication workflow.

Scheduled communication sequences

Build multi-step communication sequences for complex events — migrations, cutovers, or phased rollouts — and schedule the entire sequence in advance.

Templates library with personalisation tokens

A library of pre-approved communication templates with personalisation tokens — client name, service name, ETA, engineer — for fast, consistent communications at scale.

Multi-channel delivery

Send via HTML email, SMS, in-app notifications, or status page updates — with each contact receiving communications via their preferred channel.

Client status page

A professional, branded status page that clients can check directly — showing current incident status, planned maintenance windows, and service history.

Post-Incident Reporting

Available on all plans
AI-generated post-incident reports

Composure automatically generates a structured, professional post-incident report using the full incident timeline, communications sent, and internal notes — ready for review immediately after resolution.

Three-stage approval workflow

Post-incident reports pass through a structured approval workflow — technical review, service delivery review, and final sign-off — before reaching the client.

Professional report formatting

Reports are formatted professionally — covering root cause, timeline, impact, remediation, and preventative actions — in a structure that builds client confidence rather than undermining it.

Governance & Control

Available on all plans
Immutable audit trail

Every communication sent, every edit made, every approval given is recorded permanently. Complete accountability for every interaction with every client.

Role-based access control

Define what each team member can see, draft, approve, or send. Keep sensitive client information visible only to those who need it.

Human-in-the-loop safeguards

Nothing is ever sent without human approval. Composure is AI-assisted, not AI-autonomous. Your team remains in control of every communication.

Billing & Subscription Management

Available on all plans
Usage dashboard

A clear view of communications sent, incidents managed, and platform usage — helping you understand the value Composure is delivering and how your team is using it.

Stripe billing integration

Manage your subscription, upgrade your plan, and update billing details directly within Composure — fully integrated with Stripe for secure, reliable payment processing.

Subscription management

Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts, no exit penalties. Pricing that scales with your needs.
