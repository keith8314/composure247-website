Incident Communication

All plans

Under 3 minutes from incident detection to first client communication — with the right message for every stakeholder, automatically.

P0 and P1 incident trigger

Raise a critical incident in seconds directly within Composure or via your connected PSA. Communications begin immediately.

Severity escalation handling

As severity escalates, Composure adjusts tone, urgency, and communication frequency — keeping clients appropriately informed throughout.

Duplicate incident detection

Identifies when multiple alerts relate to the same underlying event, preventing clients from receiving confusing duplicate messages.

Multi-incident dashboard

Real-time view of all active incidents and communication status across every affected client. No incidents fall through the gaps.

Communication playbooks

Define your rules — what language to avoid, what detail to share, what tone to use — and Composure follows them on every single send.

Private internal notes

Keep internal context completely separate from client-facing communications. Internal deliberations never accidentally reach a client.

Scheduled holding updates

Composure schedules regular updates so clients know they haven't been forgotten, even when there's no new resolution progress to share.

Communication pause and resume

In sensitive situations, pause all outbound communications with one click. Resume with full context preserved when the time is right.

Sentiment checks before send

Composure reviews every draft — flagging blame language, oversharing, or tone that doesn't match your playbook standards before it leaves.

Stakeholder & Contact Management

All plans

A CTO, a Finance Director, and an IT Manager need entirely different information from the same incident. Composure knows the difference — automatically.

Customer and contact management

A complete record of every client, contact, role, and communication preference. All the context Composure needs to write the right message for the right person.

Role-based contacts and preferences

Tag each contact with their role and define preferred channel, level of detail, and communication frequency. Composure respects them every time.

Audience-calibrated AI messaging

Each message is genuinely different — not the same message adjusted slightly. Different content, tone, and level of detail per recipient role.

Per-contact previews and editing

Before sending, preview exactly what each contact will receive. Edit individual messages without affecting others — full human control before anything goes out.

Acknowledgement tracking

See who has opened and acknowledged each communication. Know immediately if a key stakeholder hasn't seen a critical update — and act before they call.

Failed delivery detection and retry

If a communication fails to deliver, Composure detects it immediately, alerts your team, and automatically retries via an alternative channel.

Internal Briefing & AM Support

Communicate & Platform

Your Account Manager should never find out about a P1 from the client. Composure briefs them automatically — before the phone rings.

AM and CSM internal briefings

Automatically brief Account Managers and CSMs at the same time client communications go out — so your relationship team is always ahead of the client's call.

Internal update chasing

Composure prompts engineers for status updates at the right intervals, using canned-response shortcuts to make internal updates fast and frictionless.

Ticket and incident data capture

Composure pulls relevant ticket context from your PSA automatically, giving the AI the raw information it needs to draft accurate, contextual communications.

Planned & Routine Communications

Communicate & Platform

Maintenance windows, migrations, service updates. Composure turns rough notes into professional branded communications in seconds — not hours.

General communications

Send professional service updates, product notices, and general communications — all managed through Composure with the same quality and consistency.

Planned maintenance notices

Schedule and send structured maintenance communications — initial notice, reminder, in-progress update, completion confirmation — as a single managed workflow.

Scheduled communication sequences

Build multi-step sequences for complex events — migrations, cutovers, phased rollouts — and schedule the entire sequence in advance.

Templates with personalisation tokens

Pre-approved templates with tokens — client name, service, ETA, engineer — for fast, consistent communications at scale. Save any sent communication as a new template.

Multi-channel delivery

Send via HTML email, SMS, in-app notifications, or status page — each contact receiving communications via their preferred channel automatically.

Client status page

A professional branded status page clients can check directly — showing current incident status, planned maintenance windows, and service history.

Post-Incident Reporting

All plans

Most MSPs take 3–7 days to produce a post-incident report. Composure generates one — ready for review — the moment an incident closes.

AI-generated post-incident reports

A structured, professional report generated from the full incident timeline, all communications sent, and internal notes — ready for review immediately after resolution.

Three-stage approval workflow

Reports pass through technical review, service delivery review, and final sign-off before reaching the client. Nothing sends without all required approvals confirmed.

Professional report formatting

Root cause, timeline, impact, remediation, and preventative actions — in a structure that builds client confidence rather than undermining it.

Governance & Control

All plans

Composure is AI-assisted, not AI-autonomous. Your team remains in control of every communication. Nothing sends without a human approving it.

Immutable audit trail

Every communication sent, every edit made, every approval given is recorded permanently. Complete accountability for every client interaction.

Role-based access control

Define what each team member can see, draft, approve, or send. Sensitive client information stays visible only to those who need it.

Human-in-the-loop safeguards

Nothing is ever sent without human approval. Composure drafts; your team decides. The AI writes it — a person sends it.
