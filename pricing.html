Pricing philosophy

Priced to make the decision easy

Composure is priced to ensure that the decision to protect your client relationships is never a difficult one. We deliberately avoid per-user pricing, annual lock-ins, and enterprise contract theatrics.

No per-user surprises

Flat monthly pricing means your cost stays predictable as your team grows. Add as many users as you need without watching the bill climb.

No annual lock-in required

Month-to-month by default. If you want to commit annually for a 20% saving, that's your choice — but we don't force it.

Start immediately

No lengthy onboarding process, no professional services engagement. Most MSPs are live and communicating professionally within a single working day.
